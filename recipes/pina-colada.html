Pina colada

A delicious pina colada

An alcohol drink with pineapple and coconut

Ingredients

  • rum
  • pineapple and coconut drink
  • ice
  • shaker
  • pineapple
  1. fill the shaker with ice
  2. add 2 ounce of rum
  3. fill the shaker with pineapple and coconut drink
  4. shake
  5. serve, and put a slice of pineapple in the glass
