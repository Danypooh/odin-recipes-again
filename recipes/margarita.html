Margarita

A delicious margarita

An alcohol drink with some tequila and lemon, al blended with ice

Ingredients

  • ice
  • blender
  • tequila
  • syrup
  • lemon
  • orange licor
  • salt
  1. fill the blender with ice
  2. add 2 ounces of tequila
  3. add 2 ounces of syrup
  4. add 2 ounces of orange licor
  5. add 3 ounces of lemon
  6. blend
  7. add salt
